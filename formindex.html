About Hotstar

Hotstar is a popular Indian over-the-top streaming service. It was launched in February 2015 and is owned by Novi Digital Entertainment, a subsidiary of Star India. Hotstar offers a wide range of content, including movies, TV shows, sports, and original programming. The platform gained immense popularity, especially for its live streaming of cricket matches and other sports events. Hotstar became the go-to platform for streaming Indian TV shows and movies, catering to a diverse audience. In 2020, Hotstar was rebranded as Disney+ Hotstar after The Walt Disney Company acquired 21st Century Fox, which owned Star India. The streaming service also features a premium subscription option, offering exclusive content and ad-free streaming. Hotstar has expanded its content library to include international shows and movies, making it a global streaming platform. It has a user-friendly interface and supports multiple languages, enhancing the viewing experience for a diverse audience. With a vast and varied content library, Hotstar continues to be a leading player in the competitive streaming industry.
